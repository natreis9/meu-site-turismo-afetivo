Tourism

Águas Claras: A Dive into Memory

If you want to explore beyond Brasília's traditional tourist spots, Águas Claras is the perfect destination. Just 20 km from the center, this administrative region has its own unique charm and offers a series of attractions that capture the daily life of its residents and visitors.

Some Memories and Places - Quiosque do Cabeça

Located on Rua 5 Sul in Águas Claras, Quiosque do Cabeça is a traditional bar that enchants with its relaxed and welcoming atmosphere. Frequented by locals, it is the ideal place to relax after a day of exploring Brasília. The highlight is its famous "jantinhas" – skewers of beef, chicken, sausage, and cheese, always accompanied by simple yet delicious dishes. If you're looking for an authentic experience, this is the place to be!

Infinu Creative Community

For those who appreciate a more alternative vibe, Infinu Creative Community is the right choice. Located at 506 Sul, Infinu combines art, fashion, gastronomy, and well-being in a collaborative and innovative environment. With cultural events, shows, and exhibitions, it is the meeting point for those who value creativity and conscious consumption in Brasília. A multifunctional space that celebrates local and independent production, perfect for those looking for something different and unique.

Exploring DF with Memories and Flavors

Affective tourism in Brasília is not limited to places, but also to the memories and experiences they provide. Throughout this tour, you will connect with the city's history and its residents, whether it's with a quiet night at a karaoke or eating the best pizza in the region. Brasília is a city made to feel, explore, and above all, remember. Allow yourself to live these affective memories and discover what is most charming in the capital of Brazil.

From Quiosque do Cabeça, Rua 5 Sul - Águas Claras, Brasília - DF, 70297-400 to Infinu Creative Community, CRS 506 Bloco A Loja 67 next to Praça das Avós, SHCS, Brasília - DF, 70350-515 You will take a metro towards Arniqueiras - Central: 25 minutes journey and an Uber: 10 minutes journey.
